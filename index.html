                  ABOUT US


Established in the year 2009, we are a manufacturer and exporter of LIGHTING and Brass, Aluminium and Stainless Steel handicrafts. Designed by expert designers, our range is offered in fascinating designs and shapes with excellent finish.


Established and located in Moradabad, Uttar Pradesh, Our well-built infrastructure and a talented workforce have helped us to provide clients with world-class antique decorative items. Being a customer-centric organization; we have directed all our endeavours towards achieving the maximum client satisfaction and thereby build a huge client base across the globe.


Design and Development:


• We have a team of professional designers for designing and developing new products. They are engaged in designing & planning the new product in hard goods field to fulfill the changing requirements of International Market & to provide best possible product to our buyers.

• In our development workshop we check the usability & performance of every product we design to provide the best quality to the end customer.

• We also welcome the buyers to design & develop their exclusive products as per their specifications.

Area of Expertise:


• Brass / Iron / Aluminum: Designer Hanging Light , Table Lamps , Wall Light Bollards, Candle / Pillar Holders, Table Tops, Gift Article etc.

• Copper: Lightings &Fittings Antique Copper Candle Holders.

• Stainless Steel: Candle / Pillar Holders, Kitchen Wares, Table Top, Gift Articles etc.



Infrastructure:


We maintain a state-of-the-art product development that includes a designing unit, sprawling manufacturing shop, well equipped packaging area and storage. The production unit is equipped with Nibbling machines, Moulding machines, Plating & Coating plants, Polishing machines, Drilling / Bending machines, Induction annealing machines, Single action press and more. The manufacturing unit is facilitated to provide comfortable working atmosphere to our workforce.
